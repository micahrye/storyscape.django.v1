About

StoryScape is a unique platform being developed to take advantage of social, mobile, and web technologies. Stories created with the Builder are meant for the StoryScape Reader, a mobile application for Android 4.0+ devices. The Reader allows the stories to truly come to life with their interactive features. Best of all the Reader allows you to explore your own creativity and imagination by adding your own personal touch to each story.

Currently the StoryScape Reader is only available to inital testers of the platform. If you are interested in becoming an early tester please contact us at sodiio.tech@gmail.com and be part of a whole new story.

If you would like to be notified when StoryScape is publicly released please contact us at sodiio.tech@gmail.com.
