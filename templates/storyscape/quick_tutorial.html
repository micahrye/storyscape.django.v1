Quick Tutorial (Under Construction)

Step 1: Getting Media

You will need images for your stories. There are two main ways to get images. Sodiio provides a "Media Library" full of images that you can "copy" to your personal library. On the top toolbar you will find a menu named "Media" which links you to the "Image Library." You can also upload your own art work, just press the "upload media" button on the top toolbar.

Step 2: Start a New Story

In the top toolbar press the "Story" menu and then select "New." A form will open that will have all of the required fileds you need to fill out to start a new story.
